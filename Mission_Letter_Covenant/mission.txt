
Mission Statement

	The purpose of the Acme Test Proctoring Services' database is to facilitate the informational needs of proctors, instructors and students at our proctoring center.

Mission Objective(s)
	
-maintain student identification information

-maintain proctor identification information and contact information

-maintain instructor identification information and contact information

-keep testing schedule

-keep track of room capacity and seating/equipment availability

-hold test information and files
